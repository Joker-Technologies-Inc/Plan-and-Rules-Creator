# Sessions Module - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### Q: What is a session?
**A:** A session is a scheduled appointment or work session with a client. Sessions can be confirmed and then added to invoices.

### Q: What's the difference between a session and a time record?
**A:** They're the same thing! "Session" and "time record" are used interchangeably in the system.

### Q: Why do some sessions need confirmation?
**A:** Sessions may need confirmation if they use manual pricing, are outside normal hours, or meet certain criteria. Confirmation ensures accuracy before invoicing.

### Q: Where do I see pending sessions?
**A:** Pending sessions appear in the "Pending Confirmation" section of the sidebar, with a count badge showing how many need confirmation.

---

## Pending Sessions

### Q: How do I confirm a pending session?
**A:** Go to the "Pending Confirmation" section in the sidebar, review the session details, and click "Confirm."

### Q: What happens when I confirm a session?
**A:** The session status changes to "Confirmed," it's removed from the pending list, and it becomes available for invoicing.

### Q: Can I confirm multiple sessions at once?
**A:** Bulk confirmation is planned for future updates. Currently, sessions must be confirmed individually.

### Q: Do I have to confirm sessions?
**A:** Yes, sessions that need confirmation must be confirmed before they can be added to invoices. This ensures accuracy.

### Q: What if I don't confirm a session?
**A:** Unconfirmed sessions remain in the pending list and cannot be invoiced until confirmed.

### Q: Can I unconfirm a session?
**A:** Once confirmed, sessions typically cannot be unconfirmed. If you need to change a confirmed session, you can edit it.

---

## Session Status

### Q: What are the different session statuses?
**A:** 
- **Scheduled**: Newly created, may need confirmation
- **Confirmed**: Approved and ready for invoicing
- **Invoiced**: Already included in an invoice

### Q: How does a session become "Invoiced"?
**A:** When you add a session to an invoice, it's automatically marked as "Invoiced" to prevent duplicate invoicing.

### Q: Can I change a session from "Invoiced" back to "Confirmed"?
**A:** No, once a session is invoiced, it cannot be changed back. This prevents duplicate invoicing.

### Q: Can I invoice a "Scheduled" session?
**A:** No, only "Confirmed" sessions can be invoiced. Confirm the session first.

### Q: Why can't I select a session for invoicing?
**A:** Possible reasons:
- Session isn't confirmed (must be "Confirmed" status)
- Session is already invoiced
- Session belongs to a different client

## Selecting Sessions for Invoices

### Q: How do I select sessions for an invoice?
**A:** When creating a session-based invoice:
1. Select the client
2. View available sessions (only confirmed, uninvoiced sessions)
3. Check the sessions you want to include
4. Review the total
5. Continue to invoice details

### Q: Which sessions can I select?
**A:** Only sessions that are:
- Confirmed (not just "Scheduled")
- Not already invoiced
- For the selected client

### Q: Can I select sessions from different clients?
**A:** No, each invoice is for one client. Select the client first, then choose sessions for that client.

### Q: Can I see session details when selecting?
**A:** Yes, you can see:
- Date and time
- Duration
- Rate or price
- Total amount

### Q: How is the total calculated when selecting sessions?
**A:** The total is the sum of all selected sessions' amounts, calculated automatically as you select/deselect.

### Q: Can I select all sessions at once?
**A:** Yes, there's typically a "Select All" option to quickly select all available sessions for a client.

## Session Management

### Q: Can I edit a session after it's confirmed?
**A:** Yes, you can edit sessions even after confirmation, but be careful if the session is already invoiced.

### Q: Can I edit a session after it's invoiced?
**A:** Editing invoiced sessions may be restricted to preserve invoice integrity. Check the invoice first if you need to make changes.

### Q: Can I delete a session?
**A:** Yes, you can delete sessions, but you may not be able to delete sessions that are already invoiced.

### Q: What happens if I delete a session that's in an invoice?
**A:** The system typically prevents deletion of invoiced sessions, or warns you, as they're part of an invoice record.

## Session Eligibility

### Q: How do I know which sessions can be invoiced?
**A:** Sessions that can be invoiced are:
- Status: "Confirmed"
- Not marked as "Invoiced"
- For the client you're invoicing

### Q: Why don't I see any sessions when creating an invoice?
**A:** Possible reasons:
- No confirmed sessions exist for the client
- All sessions are already invoiced
- Sessions are for a different client
- No sessions have been created yet

### Q: Can I invoice a session multiple times?
**A:** No, each session can only be invoiced once. Once marked as "Invoiced," it cannot be added to another invoice.

## Troubleshooting

### Q: Sessions aren't showing in the pending list
**A:** Check:
- Sessions actually need confirmation
- You're looking in the right place (sidebar)
- Try refreshing the page

### Q: I can't confirm a session
**A:** Check:
- You have permission
- The session exists
- Try refreshing and confirming again

### Q: Sessions aren't available for invoicing
**A:** Check:
- Sessions are confirmed (not just "Scheduled")
- Sessions aren't already invoiced
- Sessions are for the correct client
- Try refreshing the invoice creation page

## Best Practices

### Q: When should I confirm sessions?
**A:** Confirm sessions as soon as they're scheduled or completed. This keeps your pending list manageable and makes sessions ready for invoicing.

### Q: Should I confirm sessions immediately after creating them?
**A:** Yes, if the session doesn't need review, confirm it right away to make it available for invoicing.

### Q: How often should I check pending sessions?
**A:** Check pending sessions regularly (daily or as needed) to keep them confirmed and ready for invoicing.

### Q: Should I confirm sessions before or after the session date?
**A:** You can confirm sessions at any time - before or after the actual session date. Confirm them as soon as you're sure of the details.
