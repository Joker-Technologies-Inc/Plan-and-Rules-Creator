# Dashboard Module - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### Q: What is the Dashboard?
**A:** The Dashboard is your main overview page that shows key business metrics, recent activity, weekly earnings, and a summary of your invoices. It's the first page you see after logging in.

### Q: What information does the Dashboard show?
**A:** The Dashboard displays:
- **Total Revenue**: Your total revenue with percentage change
- **Upcoming Sessions**: Count of upcoming appointments and today's sessions
- **Active Clients**: Number of active clients and remaining slots (for free users)
- **Recent Activity**: Timeline of recent business activities
- **Weekly Earnings**: Chart showing daily earnings for the week
- **Invoice Summary**: Table of recent invoices with quick actions

### Q: How often is the Dashboard updated?
**A:** The Dashboard updates in real-time when you perform actions. You can also manually refresh the page to get the latest data.

### Q: Can I customize what appears on my Dashboard?
**A:** Currently, the Dashboard shows standard metrics. Customization options are planned for future updates.

## KPI Cards Questions

### Q: What does "Total Revenue" show?
**A:** Total Revenue displays your cumulative revenue amount and the percentage change compared to the previous period. This gives you a quick view of your business performance.

### Q: How is the percentage change calculated?
**A:** The percentage change compares your current period's revenue to the previous period (e.g., this month vs. last month).

### Q: What does "Upcoming Sessions" mean?
**A:** This shows the total number of scheduled sessions in the future, plus how many are scheduled for today specifically.

### Q: Why does "Active Clients" show remaining slots for me?
**A:** If you're on the Free plan, you'll see how many client slots you have remaining (out of 3 total). Paid users see "Unlimited clients."

## Weekly Earnings Questions

### Q: What does the Weekly Earnings chart show?
**A:** The Weekly Earnings chart displays your daily earnings for the current week (Monday through Sunday) in a visual format, making it easy to see earnings trends.

### Q: How is the earnings data calculated?
**A:** Earnings are calculated from paid invoices and completed sessions for each day of the week.

### Q: Can I see earnings for a different week?
**A:** Currently, the chart shows the current week. Options to view different weeks are planned for future updates.

### Q: Why are some days showing zero earnings?
**A:** Days with zero earnings mean no payments were received on those days. This is normal if you don't have sessions or payments every day.

## Invoice Summary Questions

### Q: What invoices appear in the summary table?
**A:** The Invoice Summary table shows your most recent invoices with key information like invoice number, client name, dates, amount, and status.

### Q: How do I send an invoice from the Dashboard?
**A:** Click the "Send" button next to an invoice in the summary table. This opens an email composition dialog where you can send the invoice to your client.

### Q: How do I send a payment reminder?
**A:** Click the "Remind" button next to an invoice. This sends a payment reminder email to your client.

### Q: Can I view full invoice details from the Dashboard?
**A:** Yes, click on an invoice row or the "View" button to see complete invoice details.

### Q: What do the invoice status colors mean?
**A:** 
- **Created**: Draft invoice (gray/blue)
- **Sent**: Invoice sent to client (yellow/orange)
- **Paid**: Invoice fully paid (green)
- **Overdue**: Past due date, not paid (red)

## Data Accuracy Questions

### Q: Why don't my numbers match what I expect?
**A:** The Dashboard shows data based on your actual records. If numbers seem incorrect, check:
- Invoice statuses (only paid invoices count toward revenue)
- Session dates (upcoming sessions are future-dated)
- Client status (only active clients are counted)

### Q: How do I refresh the Dashboard data?
**A:** Refresh your browser page or wait for automatic updates. The Dashboard updates when you perform actions in other sections.

### Q: Why is my revenue showing as zero?
**A:** Revenue is calculated from paid invoices. If you haven't received any payments yet, or if invoices are still in "Created" or "Sent" status, revenue will be zero.

## Troubleshooting

### Q: The Dashboard is loading slowly. What should I do?
**A:** This could be due to:
- Large amount of data
- Slow internet connection
- Browser cache issues

Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the problem persists, contact support.

### Q: Some cards are showing "Loading..." indefinitely
**A:** This usually indicates a connection issue. Try:
- Refreshing the page
- Checking your internet connection
- Clearing browser cache
- Contacting support if the issue continues

### Q: The Dashboard shows old data
**A:** Refresh the page to get the latest data. The Dashboard should update automatically, but a manual refresh ensures you have current information.
