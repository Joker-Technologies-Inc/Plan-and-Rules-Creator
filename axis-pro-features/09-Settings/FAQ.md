# Settings Module - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### Q: How do I access settings?
**A:** Click "Settings" in the navigation menu or navigate to `/settings` to access your account settings.

### Q: What settings are available?
**A:** Settings include:
- **Billing**: Subscription and payment management
- **Notifications**: Email and alert preferences
- Additional settings (as available)

### Q: Can I change settings on mobile?
**A:** Yes, all settings are accessible and can be changed on mobile devices.

---

## Billing Settings

### Q: How do I view my current plan?
**A:** Go to Settings → Billing to see your current subscription plan, features, and pricing.

### Q: What plans are available?
**A:** 
- **Free Plan**: $0/month - Up to 3 clients, basic features
- **Pro Plan**: $8/month - Unlimited clients, all features

### Q: How do I upgrade to Pro?
**A:** 
1. Go to Settings → Billing
2. Click "Upgrade to Pro"
3. Enter payment information
4. Complete the upgrade

### Q: What happens when I upgrade?
**A:** 
- Your plan changes to Pro immediately
- Client limit becomes unlimited
- All Pro features become available
- Billing starts for the new plan

### Q: Can I downgrade from Pro to Free?
**A:** Yes, you can downgrade, but this will:
- Limit you to 3 clients
- Remove access to Pro-only features
- You may need to reduce your client count if you have more than 3

### Q: How do I update my payment method?
**A:** 
1. Go to Settings → Billing
2. Click "Update Payment Method"
3. Enter new payment information
4. Save

### Q: What payment methods are accepted?
**A:** Credit cards and other payment methods are accepted. Specific options are shown during payment setup.

### Q: When will I be charged?
**A:** For Pro plan, you're charged monthly on your billing date. The exact date is shown in your billing settings.

### Q: Can I see my billing history?
**A:** Billing history viewing is planned for future updates. Currently, you can see your current plan and payment method.

### Q: Can I cancel my subscription?
**A:** Yes, you can cancel your Pro subscription from the billing settings. Your access continues until the end of your billing period.

## Notification Settings

### Q: How do I manage my notifications?
**A:** Go to Settings → Notifications to view and toggle notification preferences.

### Q: What notification types are available?
**A:** 
- **Invoice Reminders**: Alerts for overdue invoices
- **Appointment Reminders**: Alerts for upcoming sessions
- **New Message Alerts**: Notifications for new messages

### Q: How do I turn notifications on or off?
**A:** Toggle the switch next to each notification type to enable or disable it.

### Q: Do notification changes save automatically?
**A:** Yes, notification preferences save automatically when you toggle them.

### Q: Can I customize notification messages?
**A:** Notification message customization is planned for future updates. Currently, you can only enable/disable notification types.

### Q: Will I still receive important system notifications?
**A:** Yes, critical system notifications (like security alerts) are always sent regardless of your preferences.

### Q: Can I set quiet hours for notifications?
**A:** Quiet hours functionality is planned for future updates.

### Q: Can I receive notifications via SMS or push?
**A:** Additional notification channels (SMS, push notifications) are planned for future updates.

## Troubleshooting

### Q: I can't upgrade my plan
**A:** Check:
- Payment information is correct
- Payment method is valid
- Internet connection is stable
- Try refreshing and upgrading again
- Contact support if issues persist

### Q: Payment failed. What should I do?
**A:** 
- Check your payment method
- Ensure sufficient funds
- Update payment information if needed
- Try the upgrade again
- Contact support if problems continue

### Q: My notification settings aren't saving
**A:** Check:
- You're logged in
- Internet connection is stable
- Try refreshing and toggling again
- Contact support if issues persist

### Q: I'm not receiving notifications
**A:** Check:
- Notifications are enabled in settings
- Your email address is correct
- Check spam/junk folder
- Email service is working
- Contact support if needed

## Best Practices

### Q: How often should I review my settings?
**A:** Review your settings:
- When you first set up your account
- When your needs change
- Periodically to ensure preferences are current

### Q: Should I enable all notifications?
**A:** Enable notifications that are useful to you. You can always adjust them later based on what works best.

### Q: Should I upgrade to Pro?
**A:** Upgrade to Pro if you:
- Need more than 3 clients
- Want all features
- Need priority support
- Want advanced analytics
