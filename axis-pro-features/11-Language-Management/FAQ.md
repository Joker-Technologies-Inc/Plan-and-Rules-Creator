# Language Management Module - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### Q: What languages are supported?
**A:** Currently, the application supports:
- **English (EN)**: Default language
- **Spanish (ES)**: Secondary language

Additional languages are planned for future updates.

### Q: How do I change the language?
**A:** Click the language switcher in the top navigation bar (usually shows a flag icon), then select your preferred language from the dropdown.

### Q: Will my language preference be saved?
**A:** Yes, your language preference is saved in your browser's localStorage and will persist across sessions.

### Q: Do I need to refresh the page after changing language?
**A:** No, the language changes immediately without needing to refresh the page.

---

## Language Switching

### Q: Where is the language switcher located?
**A:** The language switcher is typically in the top navigation bar, showing a flag icon representing your current language.

### Q: How do I know which language I'm currently using?
**A:** The language switcher displays a flag icon (US for English, ES for Spanish) indicating your current language.

### Q: Can I change the language on mobile?
**A:** Yes, the language switcher works on all devices, including mobile phones and tablets.

### Q: What happens to my data when I change language?
**A:** Only the interface language changes. Your data (clients, invoices, sessions) remains the same and is not affected.

---

## Language Detection

### Q: How does the app detect my language?
**A:** On first visit, the app:
1. Checks your browser's language preference
2. If a supported language is detected, it uses that
3. Otherwise, defaults to English

### Q: Can I override the automatic language detection?
**A:** Yes, you can manually select your preferred language using the language switcher at any time.

### Q: What if my browser language isn't supported?
**A:** The app will default to English. You can then manually switch to Spanish if preferred.

## Translation Coverage

### Q: Is everything translated?
**A:** All user-facing text should be translated, including:
- Navigation menus
- Page titles
- Buttons
- Form labels
- Error messages
- Success messages
- Table headers

### Q: What if I see text that isn't translated?
**A:** If you see untranslated text, it may be:
- A new feature not yet translated
- Technical content that remains in English
- A translation that needs updating

Please report untranslated content to support.

### Q: Are email notifications translated?
**A:** Yes, email notifications are localized based on your language preference, including dates, currency, and numbers formatted according to your locale.

### Q: Are invoices translated?
**A:** Invoice content (like line items you enter) uses your input, but invoice templates and labels are translated based on your language preference.

### Q: What if I use the app on multiple devices?

## Technical Questions

### Q: Does changing language affect the URL?
**A:** URL-based language routing is planned for future updates. Currently, language is handled via localStorage and doesn't affect URLs.

### Q: Will changing language affect my data?
**A:** No, changing language only affects the interface display. All your data (clients, invoices, sessions) remains unchanged.

### Q: Can I use the app in one language and send invoices in another?
**A:** Currently, invoice language follows your interface language preference. Separate invoice language settings are planned for future updates.

### Q: What if I see a mix of languages?
**A:** This shouldn't happen. If you see mixed languages, try:
- Refreshing the page
- Changing language and changing back
- Clearing browser cache
- Contacting support if the issue persists

## Troubleshooting

### Q: The language isn't changing
**A:** Try:
- Refreshing the page
- Clearing browser cache
- Checking that you clicked the language option
- Trying a different browser
- Contacting support if issues persist

### Q: Some text is still in the wrong language
**A:** This may indicate:
- Cached content (try refreshing)
- A translation that needs updating
- New content not yet translated

Report specific instances to support.

### Q: The language switcher isn't visible
**A:** Check:
- You're logged in
- The page has loaded completely
- Try refreshing the page
- Contact support if it's consistently missing

### Q: My language preference keeps resetting
**A:** This could be due to:
- Browser clearing localStorage
- Using private/incognito mode
- Browser settings clearing data

Check your browser settings and try setting the language again.
