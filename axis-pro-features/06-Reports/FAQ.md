# Reports Module - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### Q: What types of reports are available?
**A:** Currently, you can generate:
- **Client Reports**: Detailed client activity and financial data
- **Invoice Reports**: Invoice performance and payment status

More report types are planned for future updates.

### Q: How do I generate a report?
**A:** Go to Reports, select the report type (Client or Invoice), choose a time period, and the report will generate automatically.

### Q: Can I export reports?
**A:** Export functionality is planned for future updates. You'll be able to export to CSV, Excel, and PDF formats.

### Q: How far back can I generate reports?
**A:** You can generate reports for any time period where you have data. The system supports week, month, and year timeframes.

---

## Client Reports

### Q: What information does a client report show?
**A:** Client reports include:
- Client name, email, phone
- Status (Active/Inactive)
- Total invoiced amount
- Total paid amount
- Outstanding balance
- Number of invoices
- Number of sessions

### Q: How do I generate a client report?
**A:** 
1. Go to Reports
2. Select "Client Reports" tab
3. Choose timeframe (Week, Month, Year)
4. Select specific period
5. Report generates automatically

### Q: Can I filter client reports?
**A:** You can filter by time period. Additional filtering options (by status, etc.) are planned for future updates.

### Q: What time periods can I use?
**A:** 
- **Week**: Select specific week (e.g., Week 1 of 2025)
- **Month**: Select specific month (e.g., January 2025)
- **Year**: Select specific year (e.g., 2025)

### Q: How is financial data calculated in client reports?
**A:** Financial data is calculated from all invoices and payments for each client within the selected time period.

## Invoice Reports

### Q: What information does an invoice report show?
**A:** Invoice reports include:
- Invoice number
- Client name
- Issue date
- Due date
- Total amount
- Status (Created, Sent, Paid, Overdue)
- Payment method
- Days overdue (for overdue invoices)

### Q: How do I generate an invoice report?
**A:** 
1. Go to Reports
2. Select "Invoice Reports" tab
3. Choose timeframe and period
4. Optionally filter by status
5. Report generates automatically

### Q: Can I filter invoice reports by status?
**A:** Yes, you can filter to show only invoices with specific statuses (Created, Sent, Paid, Overdue).

### Q: Can I filter by payment method?
**A:** Payment method filtering is planned for future updates.

### Q: What does "Days Overdue" mean?
**A:** This shows how many days past the due date an invoice is, calculated from the due date to today.

## Time Period Selection

### Q: How do I select a time period?
**A:** Use the timeframe selector to choose Week, Month, or Year, then select the specific period from the dropdown.

### Q: What format are periods in?
**A:** 
- **Week**: YYYY-WX (e.g., 2025-W1 for Week 1 of 2025)
- **Month**: YYYY-MM (e.g., 2025-01 for January 2025)
- **Year**: YYYY (e.g., 2025)

### Q: Can I create custom date ranges?
**A:** Custom date range selection is planned for future updates. Currently, you can select predefined weeks, months, or years.

### Q: What if I select a period with no data?
**A:** The report will show empty or show zeros. This is normal if you didn't have activity in that period.

## Report Summary Statistics

### Q: What summary statistics are shown?
**A:** Reports show:
- Total Revenue
- Uninvoiced Total
- Total Unpaid
- Total Invoices Count
- Average Invoice Amount (in some reports)

### Q: How are summary statistics calculated?
**A:** Statistics are aggregated from all data within the selected time period.

### Q: What is "Uninvoiced Total"?
**A:** This is the total value of sessions that haven't been invoiced yet.

### Q: What is "Total Unpaid"?
**A:** This is the sum of all outstanding balances on unpaid invoices.

## Troubleshooting

### Q: The report shows no data
**A:** Possible reasons:
- No data exists for the selected period
- Wrong time period selected
- Filters are too restrictive
- Try selecting a different period

### Q: The numbers don't match what I expect
**A:** Check:
- The time period is correct
- Filters aren't excluding data
- Data was entered correctly
- Calculations are based on invoice statuses (only paid invoices count toward revenue)

### Q: The report is loading slowly
**A:** This could be due to:
- Large amount of data
- Slow internet connection
- Complex calculations

Try selecting a shorter time period or refreshing the page.

### Q: I can't select a time period
**A:** Check:
- You have data in the system
- The period format is correct
- Try refreshing the page
