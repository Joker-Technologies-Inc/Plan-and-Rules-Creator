# Calendar & Time Records Module - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### Q: What is a time record?
**A:** A time record (also called a session) is a scheduled appointment or work session with a client. It includes the date, time, duration, client, and pricing information.

### Q: How do I create a time record?
**A:** Go to the Calendar page, click "Add Time Record," select a client, choose date and time, set duration, configure pricing, and save.

### Q: What's the difference between Month, Week, and Day views?
**A:** 
- **Month View**: Shows the entire month with all sessions
- **Week View**: Shows a detailed 7-day week with hourly slots
- **Day View**: Shows a single day with hour-by-hour breakdown

### Q: Can I switch between calendar views?
**A:** Yes! Use the view buttons (Month/Week/Day) to switch between different calendar perspectives.

## Creating Time Records

### Q: What information do I need to create a time record?
**A:** You need:
- Client (required)
- Date and Time (required)
- Duration (required, in minutes)
- Pricing Type (Predefined Rate or Manual Price)
- Status (Scheduled or Confirmed)

### Q: What's the difference between Predefined Rate and Manual Price?
**A:** 
- **Predefined Rate**: Select from rates you've set up for the client
- **Manual Price**: Enter a custom amount for this specific session

### Q: Can I create a time record in the past?
**A:** Yes, you can create time records for past dates. This is useful for recording sessions that weren't scheduled in advance.

### Q: Can I create multiple time records at once?
**A:** Currently, time records must be created one at a time. Bulk creation is planned for future updates.

### Q: What happens if I schedule overlapping sessions?
**A:** The system currently allows overlapping sessions. Conflict detection and warnings are planned for future updates.

## Pending Sessions

### Q: What are pending sessions?
**A:** Pending sessions are time records that need confirmation before they can be invoiced. They appear in the "Pending Confirmation" section of the sidebar.

### Q: Why do some sessions need confirmation?
**A:** Sessions may need confirmation if:
- They use manual pricing
- They're outside normal hours
- They meet certain criteria you've set

### Q: How do I confirm a pending session?
**A:** Go to the "Pending Confirmation" section in the sidebar, review the session details, and click "Confirm."

### Q: Can I confirm multiple sessions at once?
**A:** Bulk confirmation is planned for future updates. Currently, sessions must be confirmed individually.

### Q: What happens after I confirm a session?
**A:** The session status changes to "Confirmed," it's removed from the pending list, and it becomes available for invoicing.

## Editing and Managing Sessions

### Q: How do I edit a time record?
**A:** Click on the time record in the calendar to open it, then click "Edit." Make your changes and save.

### Q: Can I change the client for a time record?
**A:** Yes, you can edit the client when editing a time record.

### Q: Can I change the date and time of a session?
**A:** Yes, you can edit the date and time when editing a time record.

### Q: How do I delete a time record?
**A:** Click on the time record, then click "Delete" and confirm. The session will be removed from the calendar.

### Q: What happens if I delete a session that's already invoiced?
**A:** Sessions that are marked as "Invoiced" typically cannot be deleted, or you'll receive a warning, as they're part of an invoice.

## Session Status

### Q: What do the different session statuses mean?
**A:** 
- **Scheduled**: Newly created, may need confirmation
- **Confirmed**: Approved and ready for invoicing
- **Invoiced**: Already included in an invoice

### Q: Can I change a session from "Invoiced" back to "Confirmed"?
**A:** No, once a session is invoiced, it cannot be changed back. This prevents duplicate invoicing.

### Q: Why can't I invoice a "Scheduled" session?
**A:** Only "Confirmed" sessions can be invoiced. Confirm the session first, then you can add it to an invoice.

## Calendar Summary

### Q: What does the Calendar Summary show?
**A:** The Calendar Summary displays:
- Total sessions this month
- Upcoming sessions count
- Pending confirmations count
- Total hours scheduled

### Q: How is the summary calculated?
**A:** The summary calculates statistics based on your time records for the current month and upcoming periods.

## Troubleshooting

### Q: My time record isn't showing on the calendar
**A:** Check:
- The date is correct
- You're viewing the correct month/week/day
- The session was saved successfully
- Try refreshing the page

### Q: I can't edit a time record
**A:** Possible reasons:
- The session is already invoiced (may have restrictions)
- You don't have permission
- Try refreshing and trying again

### Q: The calendar is loading slowly
**A:** This could be due to:
- Many sessions in the view
- Slow internet connection
- Browser cache issues

Try refreshing or switching to a different view.

## Best Practices

### Q: How far in advance should I schedule sessions?
**A:** Schedule sessions as soon as they're confirmed. This helps you:
- See your availability
- Plan your schedule
- Avoid double-booking

### Q: Should I confirm sessions immediately?
**A:** Yes, confirming sessions as soon as they're scheduled makes them ready for invoicing and keeps your pending list manageable.

### Q: How should I name or describe sessions?
**A:** While sessions don't have separate names, the client name and time make them identifiable. Session notes/descriptions are planned for future updates.
