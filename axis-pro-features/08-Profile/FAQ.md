# Profile Module - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### Q: How do I access my profile?
**A:** Click on your profile menu (usually in the top navigation) or navigate to `/profile` to view your profile page.

### Q: What information is shown on my profile?
**A:** Your profile displays:
- Name
- Email address
- User role (Free, Paid, Admin)
- Account type
- Subscription status (if applicable)
- Account creation date (if available)

### Q: Can I change my profile picture?
**A:** Profile picture upload functionality is planned for future updates. Currently, a default avatar is used.

## Editing Profile

### Q: How do I edit my profile information?
**A:** Click "Edit Profile" on your profile page, make your changes, and click "Save."

### Q: What can I edit in my profile?
**A:** You can edit:
- Name
- Email address
- Other profile fields (as available)

### Q: Can I change my email address?
**A:** Yes, you can update your email address. You may need to verify the new email address.

### Q: What happens if I change my email to one that's already in use?
**A:** You'll see an error message saying the email is already in use. Use a different email address or contact support if needed.

### Q: Will changing my email affect my account?
**A:** Your account data remains the same. Only the email address changes. You may need to verify the new email.

## Password Management

### Q: How do I change my password?
**A:** Go to your Profile page, click "Change Password," enter your current password, then enter and confirm your new password.

### Q: What are the password requirements?
**A:** Your password should be:
- At least 8 characters long
- Strong (mix of letters, numbers, symbols recommended)

### Q: Do I need to know my current password to change it?
**A:** Yes, you must enter your current password to verify your identity before setting a new password.

### Q: What if I forget my current password?
**A:** Use the "Forgot Password" feature on the login page instead of the profile password change.

### Q: How often should I change my password?
**A:** We recommend changing your password every 3-6 months or immediately if you suspect your account has been compromised.

### Q: Can I use the same password I had before?
**A:** For security, it's recommended to use a new password when changing it, though the system may allow reusing previous passwords.

### Q: What happens after I change my password?
**A:** Your password is updated immediately. You may be asked to log in again with your new password for security.

## Account Security

### Q: How do I keep my account secure?
**A:** 
- Use a strong, unique password
- Change your password regularly
- Don't share your password
- Keep your email address up to date
- Log out on shared devices

### Q: What should I do if I think my account is compromised?
**A:** 
1. Change your password immediately
2. Check your account activity
3. Contact support if needed

### Q: Is my personal information secure?
**A:** Yes, we use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information and account data.

## Troubleshooting

### Q: I can't edit my profile
**A:** Check:
- You're logged in
- All required fields are filled
- Email format is correct
- Try refreshing and editing again

### Q: Profile changes aren't saving
**A:** Check:
- All required fields are filled
- Email is in correct format (if changed)
- Internet connection is stable
- Try refreshing and saving again

### Q: I can't change my password
**A:** Check:
- Current password is correct
- New password meets requirements
- Passwords match in both fields
- Try refreshing and changing again

### Q: The email I want to use is already taken
**A:** This means another account is using that email. Use a different email address or contact support if you believe this is an error.

## Account Deletion

### Q: Can I delete my account?
**A:** Account deletion functionality is available through your profile settings or by contacting support.

### Q: What happens when I delete my account?
**A:** Account deletion is permanent and removes:
- Your account
- Your personal information
- Your data (depending on retention policies)

### Q: Can I recover my account after deletion?
**A:** No, account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure you want to delete before proceeding.

### Q: Will my invoices and data be deleted?
**A:** This depends on data retention policies. Contact support for details about what happens to your data upon account deletion.
