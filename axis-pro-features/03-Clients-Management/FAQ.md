# Clients Management Module - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### Q: How do I add a new client?
**A:** Go to the Clients page, click "Add Client," fill in the client's name, email, and phone number, then click "Save." A primary contact will be automatically created with the client's information.

### Q: What information do I need to add a client?
**A:** You need:
- Client Name (required)
- Email Address (required)
- Phone Number (required)
- Status (Active/Inactive, defaults to Active)

### Q: Can I add multiple contacts for one client?
**A:** Yes! After creating a client, you can add additional contacts in the client's details page. Each client can have multiple contacts.

### Q: What's the difference between a client and a contact?
**A:** A client is the main account/organization. Contacts are individuals associated with that client. When you create a client, a primary contact is automatically created.

## Client Limits

### Q: How many clients can I have?
**A:** 
- **Free Plan**: Up to 3 active clients
- **Paid Plan**: Unlimited clients

### Q: What happens when I reach my client limit?
**A:** If you're on the Free plan and reach 3 clients, you'll see a message indicating you've reached your limit. You can either:
- Upgrade to Paid plan for unlimited clients
- Mark an existing client as "Inactive" to free up a slot

### Q: Do inactive clients count toward my limit?
**A:** Only active clients count toward your limit. Inactive clients don't use up your available slots.

## Managing Clients

### Q: How do I edit a client's information?
**A:** Click on the client in the list, then click "Edit." Make your changes and click "Save."

### Q: Can I change a client's email address?
**A:** Yes, you can edit the client's email. The system will check that the new email isn't already in use.

### Q: What happens if I try to add a client with an email that already exists?
**A:** You'll see an error message saying "Email already exists." You can search for the existing client or use a different email address.

### Q: How do I delete a client?
**A:** Click "Delete" on the client, confirm the deletion, and the client will be removed. Note: You may not be able to delete clients with associated invoices or sessions.

### Q: What happens to invoices and sessions when I delete a client?
**A:** The system checks for associated data before allowing deletion. You may need to handle invoices and sessions first, or the system may prevent deletion to preserve data integrity.

## Contacts Management

### Q: How do I add a contact to a client?
**A:** Open the client's details, go to the Contacts section, click "Add Contact," enter the contact's information, and save.

### Q: Can I have multiple contacts with the same email?
**A:** While technically possible, it's recommended to use unique emails for each contact to avoid confusion.

### Q: How do I edit a contact?
**A:** In the client's Contacts section, click "Edit" on the contact you want to modify, make changes, and save.

### Q: How do I delete a contact?
**A:** Click "Delete" on the contact and confirm. The primary contact (created with the client) typically cannot be deleted.

### Q: What's the primary contact?
**A:** The primary contact is automatically created when you add a client. It uses the client's main information. You can edit it, but it's usually the main point of contact.

## Searching and Filtering

### Q: How do I search for a client?
**A:** Use the search bar at the top of the Clients page. You can search by name, email, or phone number.

### Q: Can I filter clients by status?
**A:** Yes, you can filter to show only Active or Inactive clients using the filter options.

### Q: Can I sort the client list?
**A:** Yes, you can sort by clicking on column headers (name, email, status, etc.).

## Client Financial Information

### Q: What does "Total Invoiced" mean?
**A:** This is the sum of all invoice amounts created for this client.

### Q: What does "Total Paid" mean?
**A:** This is the total amount of payments received from this client.

### Q: What is "Outstanding Balance"?
**A:** This is the amount the client still owes: Total Invoiced minus Total Paid.

### Q: How is the financial information calculated?
**A:** Financial data is automatically calculated from invoices and payments associated with the client.

### Q: Why is my client's financial information showing zero?
**A:** Financial information only appears after you've created invoices for the client. New clients will show zeros until invoices are generated.

## Client Rates

### Q: Can I set different rates for different clients?
**A:** Yes! You can assign specific rates to clients. Go to the client's details and manage their rates in the Rates section.

### Q: How do I assign a rate to a client?
**A:** In the client's details, go to the Rates section, select a rate from your available rates, and assign it to the client.

### Q: Can I create a custom rate for a specific client?
**A:** Yes, you can create client-specific rates. These will only apply to that particular client.

## Client Status

### Q: What's the difference between Active and Inactive clients?
**A:** 
- **Active**: Client is currently working with you, can be assigned to sessions, and appears in active lists
- **Inactive**: Client is archived, won't appear in active client counts, but data is preserved

### Q: How do I change a client's status?
**A:** Edit the client and change the Status field from Active to Inactive (or vice versa).

### Q: Should I mark old clients as Inactive?
**A:** Yes, marking clients you're no longer working with as Inactive helps keep your active client list clean and can free up slots on the Free plan.

## Troubleshooting

### Q: I can't add a new client. Why?
**A:** Possible reasons:
- You've reached your client limit (Free plan users)
- The email address is already in use
- Required fields are missing
- Network/connection issue

Check your client count and try again. If the problem persists, contact support.

### Q: The client search isn't working
**A:** Try:
- Clearing the search and typing again
- Checking for typos
- Refreshing the page
- Using a different search term (name vs. email)

### Q: Client information isn't saving
**A:** Check:
- All required fields are filled
- Email format is correct
- You have a stable internet connection
- Try refreshing and saving again

## Best Practices

### Q: How should I organize my clients?
**A:** 
- Keep client information up to date
- Mark inactive clients as Inactive
- Add multiple contacts for clients with teams
- Use consistent naming conventions
- Keep email addresses current

### Q: Should I delete old clients or mark them inactive?
**A:** We recommend marking clients as Inactive rather than deleting them. This preserves your history and data while keeping your active list clean.

### Q: How often should I update client information?
**A:** Update client information whenever it changes (new email, phone number, etc.). Keeping information current ensures invoices and communications go to the right place.
